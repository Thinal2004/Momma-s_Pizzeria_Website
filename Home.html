About Us

At Momma’s Pizzeria, we speak the language of love, laughter, and really good pizza. Inspired by the heart of Italy and served with the soul of Sri Lanka, our kitchen brings you the best of both worlds-traditional recipes, handcrafted dough, rich sauces, and the warmth of momma’s touch.

Every bite is a tribute to old-world Italian charm, blended with the island’s flair for hospitality and spice. Whether you're here for a quick slice or a family feast, you're not just a customer-you’re part of the Momma’s family. Come hungry, leave happy

What We Sell
